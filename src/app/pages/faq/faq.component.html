General FAQs on the Software

+
To create an order, log in to the software and navigate to the "Create Order" section. Fill in the required patient details, choose the specific dental procedure or product, and attach any relevant digital files. Once the order details are complete, submit it through the system, and it will be assigned to the appropriate department for processing.
+
Tracking orders is easy with our software. Simply access the "Order Status" or "Case Tracking" feature. Here, you can view real-time updates on each stage of the production process. From case design to manufacturing and delivery, the software provides a detailed overview of your orders, ensuring transparency and timely completion.
+
The software is designed for dental professionals, including technicians, administrators, and managers within the dental laboratory. User roles can be customized based on responsibilities. Technicians may have access to case details and production workflows, while administrators can oversee overall operations and manage billing. This ensures that each user has the right level of access for their role.
+
To manage billing and invoicing, go to the "Billing" or "Financial" section. Here, you can generate invoices based on completed orders, track payments, and view outstanding balances. The software automates the invoicing process, reducing the chances of errors and ensuring accurate financial records.
